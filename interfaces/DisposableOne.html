    Interface DisposableOne

    DisposableOne is an IDisposable that manages a single Disposer or IDisposable.

    The current Disposer and IDisposable in DisposableOne will be invoked(flush) when setting a new one.

    A DisposableOne is also a Disposer, which means it can be the dispose method of an IDisposable.
