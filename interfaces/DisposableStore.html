    Interface DisposableStore

    A Disposable Store is an IDisposable that manages Disposers and IDisposables.

    All Disposers and IDisposables in the store will be invoked(flush) when the store is disposed.

    A DisposableStore is also a Disposer, which means it can be the dispose method of an IDisposable.

    A DisposableStore is also an IDisposable, which means it can be managed by another DisposableStore.
